MonaLisa Touch

MonaLisa Touch uses fractional CO2 laser technology to rejuvenate vaginal health by promoting new collagen production, increasing blood flow, and restoring tissue elasticity. It addresses various conditions such as:

  • Vaginal Atrophy (atrophic vaginitis)
  • Genitourinary Syndrome of Menopause (GSM)
  • Dyspareunia (painful intercourse)
  • Postpartum Vaginal Changes
  • Alternative for Women Who Cannot Use Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT)

The MonaLisa Touch Procedure

The treatment is quick, typically taking 5-10 minutes per session, with most women requiring three sessions, spaced 4-6 weeks apart for optimal results. Benefits include:

  • Non-Hormonal
  • Non-Invasive
  • Quick and Painless
  • Minimal Downtime
  • Long-Lasting Results
